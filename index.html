Oklahoma Action Chorus

is an all-ages community space that centers discussion of a better world by celebrating anthems of working peoples' movements

Do I need singing experience to join?

No! Anyone can join the chorus, no matter your level of experience. You don't even have to know how to read music! Our director is a trained vocal teacher and has experience teaching all levels and ages.

When and where are the rehearsals?

Rehearsals are weekly on Thursdays from 5pm-7pm, and we meet at a central location in OKC.

What sorts of music will we sing?

The OAC centers music from revolutionary and progressive movements around the globe.

Examples of some movements include the American labor movement, African American civil rights movement, Latin American liberation movements, global indigenous rights movements, the US anti-war movements, and contemporary climate and social justice, like the fight for 2SLGBTQIA+ liberation.

Will we perform in concerts and at protests?

The group will be prepared with a variety of songs for a variety of events. Our goal is to be a large enough group to be flexibly available in solidarity for protest events as well as perform concerts once or twice a year. Our performance calendar will develop as we grow.

How do I join?

Joining the Oklahoma Action Chorus is easy. Fill out the inquiry form, and we will reach out to you over email to give you necessary information.
